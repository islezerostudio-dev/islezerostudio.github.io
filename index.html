Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you play our mobile games.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers (IDFA/ADID).
  • Gameplay Data: Scores, progress, in-game achievements, and logs.
  • In-App Purchase: Purchase history and receipt validation tokens.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the game service.
  • To respond to customer support inquiries.
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activity.
  • (If applicable) To serve personalized advertisements.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request data deletion at any time by contacting us via email or using the in-game delete account feature.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools for analytics and advertising:

  • Google (Firebase, AdMob)
  • Unity (Unity Ads, Analytics)
